About Us

Primary Care · Hicksville, NY

A practice built on trust & compassion

Founded by Dr. Nisreen Ezzi, MZF Medical Services was built with a clear mission: to be a one-stop-care solution for families seeking comprehensive, high-quality primary care in the Hicksville community.

With more than 20 years of healthcare experience, Dr. Ezzi and her team are deeply committed to the health and wellbeing of every patient — making each visit comfortable, thorough, and meaningful.

What We Offer

Comprehensive primary care services

From routine annual physicals to ongoing chronic disease management, our team provides a full spectrum of care for adults at every stage of life.

🩺

Physical Exams

Annual physicals, employment physicals, university & college physicals, school forms, and camp forms for all ages.

🚛

CDL DOT Physicals

Federal DOT physical examinations for commercial driver's license (CDL) holders and applicants, performed by a certified medical examiner.

💊

Chronic Disease Management

Ongoing care for high blood pressure, high cholesterol, diabetes, asthma, and other long-term conditions.

🤒

Acute Illness Treatment

Prompt evaluation and treatment for cough, cold, bronchitis, fevers, pneumonia, allergies, UTIs, and more.

✈️

Travel & STD Counseling

Pre-travel health consultations, destination-specific vaccinations, and confidential STD counseling and testing.

🧪

Pre-Op Clearance & Skin Testing

Pre-operative medical clearance for surgical procedures and Tuberculin (TB) skin testing.

🧠

Mental Health

Compassionate mental health evaluations, support, and coordinated referrals as part of your whole-person care.

👁️

Eye, Ear & Skin Conditions

Diagnosis and treatment of eye infections, ear infections, acne, skin rashes, dizziness, and fainting.

🔬

Referrals & Care Coordination

Coordinated referrals to trusted specialists and seamless communication across your full care team.
